    E-PEDAL, ONE-PEDAL DRIVING

    Nissan LEAF's e-Pedal gives you control – and excitement – like never before. It's an easier way to drive, allowing you to accelerate and decelerate with a single, seamless movement. Simply release the accelerator for automatic regenerative braking and depress again for acceleration. And if you're having too much fun or need a more immediate stop, you can always switch to the familiar brake pedal.
